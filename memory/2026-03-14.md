### Key Findings from Search

**Failure Modes Identified**:
1. **Type I Error**: Structure fails to fold into proper monomeric state
2. **Type II Error**: Structure folds but fails to form intended binding mode
3. **Expression failure**: 10-30% fail to express despite computational validation
4. **Misfolding**: High pLDDT scores but alternative conformations in solution
5. **Off-target binding**: High affinity but low specificity
6. **Stability-affinity tradeoffs**: Affinity improvements destabilize scaffold

**Success Rate Context**:
- RFdiffusion: 7-35% (depending on target class)
- BindCraft: 10-100% (varying by target)
- AlphaProteo: 3-300× improvement over baseline (but still <50% for challenging targets)
